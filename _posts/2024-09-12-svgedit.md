# svgEdit

![](../_asset/2024-09-12svgedit_image_1.jpg)

**SVGEdit** is a fast, web-based, JavaScript-driven SVG drawing editor that works in any modern browser. **SVGEdit** is based on a powerful SVG canvas **@svgedit/svgcanvas**
